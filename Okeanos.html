Okeanos

Okeanos is a strategic naval combat game where the player controls a single ship and is tasked with defeating as many enemy ships as possible. The game also includes a wind mechanic indicated by the red arrow which will drastically change each ships movement depending on its sail being down, half mast, or full mast. Before creating the game itself, we developed our own game engine called Zephyr using C++.
